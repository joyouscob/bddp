E-Waste

What is E-Waste?

BDDP supports e-waste collection initiatives in the community. These collections reduce the volume of equipment that finds its way into the county landfill by collecting and recycling items such as cell phones, MP3 players, I/T equipment, cameras, GPS units, clock radios, TVs, monitors, displays, digital photo frames, calculators, etc. BDDP's efforts have kept tens of thousands of pounds of equipment out of the landfill, reducing levels of harmful metals and other components in the waste stream.

Our First E-Waste Drive
